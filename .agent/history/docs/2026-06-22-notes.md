# Notes: Danh gia du an xu ly hop dong - bang ke

## Tom tat

Du an hien tai da la mot MVP kha gan voi yeu cau trong de bai, nhung chua the coi la dap ung day du de nghiem thu that. App da co cac workflow chinh va mot phan logic nghiep vu quan trong, tuy nhien van con mot so diem lech yeu cau, rui ro du lieu va thieu kiem chung ky thuat.

## Da dap ung

- Co 4 tab chinh: cau hinh/master data, Hop dong luan chuyen, Hop dong moi, Bang ke.
- Co upload va luu 3 master data bang `localStorage`, thong qua lop `dbService`, co huong de sau nay thay bang Google Sheets.
- Co thuat toan keyword matching cho Ma vu viec / San pham voi `confidenceScore`, trang thai can kiem tra va danh sach ung vien.
- Workflow Hop dong luan chuyen da xu ly ma khach, bo phan thuc hien, `ma_vv`, `tk_doanh thu`, chuyen trang import, san pham import va loc Fast theo logic chinh.
- Workflow Bang ke da co parse lich dang, ngay hop dong tu ma booking, VAT tu dong VAT va xuat 2 file `import_hop_dong_moi.xlsx` / `import_hop_dong_cu.xlsx`.
- UI cac workflow co cho sua inline va autocomplete cho cac truong rui ro nhu ma khach, bo phan, ma vu viec, san pham, tai khoan doanh thu.

## Chua dat / can sua

- Chua co Google Sheets database that, moi co abstraction va luu tam tren trinh duyet.
- Chua co test tu dong cho cac logic quan trong: keyword match, parse ngay bang ke, loc Fast, mapping va export 36 cot.

## Da xu ly trong lan cap nhat nay

- Da sua workflow Hop dong moi to lay ca dong chua co trong Fast va dong co trong Fast nhung `Trang thai = 2`.
- Da cap nhat `requiredHeaders` mac dinh trong `src/App.tsx` theo cac cot cot loi cua de bai.
- Da tach helper `buildFastImportRows` dung chung cho output 36 cot cua 3 workflow.
- Da chinh output Hop dong luan chuyen va Hop dong moi de cot `stt` de trong.
- Da giu output Bang ke co `stt` tuan tu nhu logic cu.
- Da dong nhat field noi bo trong workflow Hop dong moi sang camelCase cho `ngayHd1..6` va `tienHd1..6`.

## Kiem chung ky thuat

- Da cai dependencies bang `npm install`.
- Da chay thanh cong `npm run lint`.
- Da chay thanh cong `npm run build`.
- Da smoke test `http://localhost:3000` tra ve HTTP 200.
- Luu y: `npm install` co canh bao Node version cua `@vitejs/plugin-react` va 1 high severity vulnerability tu `npm audit`.

## De xuat uu tien sua

1. Bo sung test fixture Excel mau cho 3 workflow va cac case keyword/date/status quan trong.
2. Nang cap keyword matching de luu va uu tien candidate theo tung dong khi nguoi dung sua autocomplete.
3. Tach exception rules rieng cho Chuyen trang import neu danh sach ngoai le phat trien dai.
4. Xu ly tich hop Google Sheets sau khi nghiep vu local da on dinh.
5. Chay `npm audit` va quyet dinh cach xu ly vulnerability neu can dua app vao moi truong that.
